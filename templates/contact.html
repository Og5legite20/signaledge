Report a Signal Issue

If a signal appears incorrect, outdated, or if you notice a "Data unavailable" message on any market pair, please email us with the following details so we can investigate quickly:

  • The asset name (e.g., BTC/USD, EUR/USD, USD/NGN)
  • The signal or error message displayed
  • The approximate time and date (UTC) when you noticed the issue

We take data accuracy seriously and investigate all reported issues as a priority. In most cases, data issues are caused by temporary outages at upstream data providers and resolve automatically within one refresh cycle.

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new market pair, a different technical indicator, or a site feature that would make SignalEdge more useful to you? We genuinely want to hear it. Methodology Questions

If you'd like to understand how a particular signal is generated — which indicator triggered it, how the confidence score is calculated, or how the 7-day momentum threshold is determined — you're welcome to reach out directly. We are committed to transparency about our analytical methodology and will explain any calculation in plain language on request.

You can also visit our Education section for a detailed, plain-English breakdown of the RSI, Moving Averages, and other indicators used in our signal engine.
