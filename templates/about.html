What We Do

Every five minutes, SignalEdge pulls live price data from global financial markets — including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), BNB, Solana (SOL), EUR/USD, GBP/USD, and USD/NGN — and runs it through a set of well-established technical indicators to generate clear, readable trading signals.

For each market pair you get a directional signal (STRONG BUY, BUY, NEUTRAL, SELL, or STRONG SELL), a confidence percentage that reflects how strongly the indicators align, and the specific technical reasoning behind each call — all in a single view, updated automatically every five minutes without any action required from you.

Our Methodology

SignalEdge uses two of the most proven and widely respected indicators in technical analysis:

  • RSI — Relative Strength Index (14-period): A momentum oscillator that measures the speed and magnitude of recent price changes on a scale of 0 to 100. RSI readings below 30 indicate that an asset may be oversold — a potential buy opportunity. Readings above 70 suggest the asset may be overbought — a potential sell signal. RSI is used by institutional and retail traders alike to identify turning points in price action before they become obvious on a price chart.
  • Moving Averages (MA7 and MA14): A 7-day simple moving average and a 14-day simple moving average help identify the prevailing market trend and smooth out short-term volatility. When the 7-day MA crosses above the 14-day MA, it creates a pattern known as a golden cross — typically associated with strengthening upward momentum. The reverse crossing is a bearish signal. The relationship between the current price and these averages confirms whether the RSI reading aligns with the actual trend direction.

By combining RSI with moving average analysis, SignalEdge aims to surface only high-conviction signals while filtering out short-term noise that frequently misleads less experienced traders.

Our Data Sources

All market price data is sourced from publicly available, reputable financial data providers with proven track records of accuracy:

  • Yahoo Finance — Cryptocurrency OHLC (open, high, low, close) data for BTC, ETH, BNB, and SOL
  • Frankfurter API (sourced from the European Central Bank) — EUR/USD and GBP/USD daily reference rates
  • fawazahmed0 Currency API — USD/NGN current rate and 7-day historical exchange rate data
